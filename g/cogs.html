COGS | Rutgers Game Dev Community
C.O.G.S.
The Rutgers University Creation Of Games Society

We meet in room 248 in the Hill Mathematical Center's Annex on Wednesday nights from 6pm until 8pm.

MISSION: To make games we want to play and help others make games too

We are "platform agnostic" but we're not strangers to GameMaker, py-game, blender, Unity and browser based games.

Global Game Jam 2014 is something some of us participated in and loved, check it out! Our game was called: Focal Points: Download

RESOURCES: IRC chatrooms are a good way to get immediate help and discuss gamedev some rooms I've found helpful are:
    #ludumdare on irc.afternet.org
    #bbg on irc.freenode.net
    #reddit-gamedev on irc.freenode.net
